Notepad Renderer

A small game injected into the legacy Windows Notepad (win10 era) that renders at 60 FPS with no flicker.

How it works

  • Injects into notepad.exe and hooks the window procedure
  • Keyboard hook for input (WASD)
  • Immediate-mode style rendering into the Notepad text buffer
  • Avoids the classic Notepad renderer flicker
